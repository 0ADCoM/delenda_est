WAR ELEPHANTS
- Huge beasts from Africa and India, trained for war. Available to: Mauryans, Ptolemies, Seleucids, Macedonians, Persians, and Carthaginians.

- High attack strength, with a "Fear" aura that reduces the strength of nearby enemy soldiers and a "Trample" aura that reduces the health of nearby enemy units. Special bonuses against cavalry.

- Vulnerable to javelin infantry and sword infantry to a lesser extent.

- Flaming pigs can be used to frighten elephants and reduce their effectiveness by -25%.
